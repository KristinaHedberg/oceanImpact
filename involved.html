GET INVOLVED WITH OCEAN IMPACT

Whether you volunteer in your hometown to help keep our shores clean and safe, or contribute the funds that keep Ocean Impact Organisation going, there are a number of ways you can be a part of the Ocean Impact movement.

Make A Donation

Donations made to Ocean Impact Organisation of $2 or more are tax deductible.

At Ocean Impact we rely on the generosity of our supporters who donate the funds necessary to operate our campaigns to invest in planet ocean.Every contribution helps us on our mission of a sustainable future.

Join a Beach Clean-Up

Each month, Ocean Impact Organisation host local beach and waterway clean-ups. Get involved through our socials, and join a beach clean up next month!

Become A Mentor

Do you have skills and experience to help grow the ocean impact sector? Are you passionate about helping people start, grow and invest in ocean impact businesses?

Become An Investor

Are you an interested investor,philanthropist or corporate partner? Do you want to help build Australia's first ocean impact innovation ecosystem and startup accelerator?
